Cartridge Calculator

Use this tool to calculate how many cartridges you can load given the amount of materials you have available.

To use the tool, enter the amount of each material in the provided field, select how many grains of powder will be used for the cartridge you wish to load, and then choose the appropriate case from the dropdown. The tool is currently limited to pistol calibers only.
